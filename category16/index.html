Political Backlash Against Elon Musk Intensifies Amid Tech & Media Influence

Published: April 11, 2025 | By: LV33 Global News

Tech billionaire Elon Musk is facing heightened political scrutiny across the globe as lawmakers, regulators, and public figures raise concerns about his growing influence over sectors including space, artificial intelligence, electric vehicles, and digital media.

⚖️ Government Concerns

In the United States, a bipartisan group of senators has launched an inquiry into Musk’s ownership of SpaceX, Tesla, Neuralink, and X (formerly Twitter), citing concerns over monopoly power, misinformation control, and national security.

🌍 Global Criticism

  • 🇪🇺 EU regulators are investigating Musk’s AI startup xAI for violations of the Digital Services Act.
  • 🇨🇳 Chinese officials have questioned Starlink’s satellite coverage near sensitive areas.
  • 🇨🇦 Canadian MPs criticized Musk’s media ventures for enabling misinformation and political bias.

🧠 The AI Debate

Musk’s controversial statements on artificial intelligence and his opposition to certain regulatory frameworks have sparked fierce debate. Critics argue that his approach lacks accountability, while supporters claim he’s a needed check on government overreach in tech.

🎙️ Musk Responds

“I’m working on technologies that expand freedom, not limit it,” Musk posted on X. He further stated that centralized control over media and AI is “far more dangerous than any one person.”

The political pushback is expected to intensify as global elections approach and digital platforms play an even greater role in shaping public discourse.
