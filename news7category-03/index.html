  • Enhanced Licensing Requirements: Operators must meet higher standards for financial transparency, operational security, and underage gambling prevention. KSA will monitor compliance closely and issue fines or suspensions for non-compliance.
  • Stronger Consumer Protection: The new measures ensure secure payment methods, fair gaming practices, and mandatory self-exclusion programs to protect players from gambling addiction.
  • Crackdown on Unlicensed Operators: The KSA will pursue legal action against unlicensed gambling services and block illegal websites operating in the Netherlands.
  • Anti-Money Laundering Measures: Operators will need to verify player identities and monitor financial transactions for suspicious activity, contributing to the prevention of money laundering.
  • Advertising and Marketing Restrictions: Gambling ads will be more strictly regulated, limiting how and when they can be displayed to prevent targeting minors and vulnerable populations.

What This Means for Online Casinos and Players:
